Our Story

More Than a Sport — A Way of Life

Founded in 2023 on the sun-drenched shores of Delray Beach, our club was born from a shared passion for the water and the sport of rowing. We believe that the rhythm of the oar teaches discipline, the crew teaches brotherhood, and the river teaches patience.

From competitive athletes chasing championship titles to first-time rowers discovering their potential, our club welcomes every level. We are rooted in the South Florida community and proud to represent the spirit of Del Rey on waters near and far.

Our coaches are nationally certified with decades of experience, guiding athletes through technique, strength, and the unbreakable bond of team rowing.

Indoor Rowing Programs

No water? No problem. Our indoor rowing facility is open year-round with state-of-the-art Concept2 ergometers and expert instruction.

🏋️
Fitness Rowing

A full-body cardio experience designed for all fitness levels. Build strength, endurance, and technique in a motivating group environment without ever touching the water.
